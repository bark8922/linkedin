## 2. Patient, not clever
**Pillar 5 (small true things), with a real argument hiding in it**

> My receipt automation runs every two hours. It ran at midnight. It ran at 2am, and again at 4am, and it ran twice on Sunday morning while I was asleep.
>
> Almost none of those runs do anything. Nobody emails a receipt at 4am on a Sunday.
>
> I didn't schedule it that way for a clever reason. I scheduled it that way because "every two hours, forever" was easier to think about than "when would a person actually do this."
>
> That's most automation, if I'm honest about it. Not intelligence. Just something patient enough to keep checking.

**Source:** Receipts Workflow (n8n), 2-hourly trigger, 360 executions logged. Confirmed runs at 00:00, 02:00, 04:00 UTC on 12, 13 and 14 July, including through the weekend.
**Unverified:** nothing.
**Why it works:** it's funny, it costs him nothing to admit, and "patient, not clever" is a genuine counter to the agent hype without ever mentioning the agent hype.
